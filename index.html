Solar Panel Installation Workshop Just added!

Friday: September 22nd, 2018 | 9am-12pm

About:

Want to learn how to install solar? Maybe you even want to become certified to work in the solar field! You will work with a working PV system, dismantling and reinstalling it, troubleshooting and ensuring its proper operation. This is an introductory course. We assume you have little or no background in electrical work. And while there is some math involved, it is basic addition, subtraction, multiplication and division.
